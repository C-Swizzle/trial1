Steps to make a PB&J sandwich

  1. Get bread, peanut butter, jelly, and a knife.
  2. Open peanut butter jar and jelly jar
  3. Open bread bag, pull out two pieces of bread
  4. Stick knife into peanut butter jar
  5. Pull knife with peanut butter out of jar, slather knife on bread
  6. If there is enough peanut butter, move on to step 7. Otherwise, repeat steps 4 and 5.
  7. Stick knife into jelly jar
  8. Pull knife with jelly out of jar, slather knife on bread
  9. If there is enough jelly, move on to step 10. Otherwise, repeat steps 7 and 8.
  10. Take unslathered bread and put it on top of slathered bread.
  11. Eat.
